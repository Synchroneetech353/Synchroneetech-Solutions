Support Services

"ERPNext support systems center around a built-in Helpdesk/Support module that manages customer issues from creation to resolution via tickets, integrating emails/calls, automating assignments (round-robin), tracking SLAs, and providing analytics for performance improvement. Ticket Management: Centralizes customer queries (email, phone, web) into trackable support tickets. Multi-Channel Intake: Converts emails to tickets automatically and integrates with phone systems (like Twilio). Automation: Sets up rules for auto-assignment (load balancing/round-robin) and auto-closing tickets. SLA Management: Tracks response and resolution times against defined Service Level Agreements. Warranty & Maintenance: Manages warranty claims, service contracts, and schedules maintenance visits linked to specific serial numbers. Reporting & Analytics: Offers dashboards and reports (Issue Summary, First Response Time) for performance insights. "
