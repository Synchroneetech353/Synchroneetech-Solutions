Plan Maintainance

The SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) module provides a comprehensive solution for managing the upkeep, performance, and reliability of enterprise assets. It enables organizations to streamline maintenance processes through effective planning, scheduling, and execution of preventive, predictive, and corrective maintenance activities. With real-time visibility into equipment status, maintenance history, and resource allocation, SAP PM helps reduce downtime, optimize asset life cycles, and improve operational efficiency. Its seamless integration with other SAP modules—such as Materials Management, Production Planning, and Quality Management—ensures smooth coordination across departments, empowering businesses to maintain high productivity and achieve long-term cost savings.
